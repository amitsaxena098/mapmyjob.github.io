The Idea

This website is completely dedicated to job seekers of Bangalore. This platform makes it very easy to look for a job in your neighbourhood, or in your city itself.


Ever missed a walkin near your place while looking for other ?

"WE DID. A HELL LOT OF TIMES"

Sometimes I go for some walkin, get rejected in the first round itself ( LOL! ), and then go back to my PG to look for walkins that are taking place the next day.

Nope. Thats where we do mistake, why waste the whole day when you can try yourself in other nearby running walkins?


Open the website, Click on "Track My Location", and there you go. You will see the "JOB" icons if any walkin is happening or about to happend around you.

Hope this small idea will bring some easeness in you job seeking moments. Besides we are trying our hard to keep improving this tool, and will gather as much job walkin details as we can.
